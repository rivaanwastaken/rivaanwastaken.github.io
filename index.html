About Table Tennis

Table Tennis, also known as Ping Pong, is a fast-paced sport that is enjoyed by people of all ages. It requires quick reflexes, precision, and a lot of skill.

Table Tennis Rules

The basic rules of table tennis involve scoring points by hitting the ball over the net so that your opponent cannot return it. The first player to 11 points wins the game.
