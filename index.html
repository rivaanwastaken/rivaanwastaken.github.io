Table Tennis Rules

  • The game is played to 11 points. The first player to reach 11 points wins.
  • Players alternate serves every two points, except in deuce situations.
  • The ball must bounce on both sides of the table for a valid serve.
  • Games are typically played best out of 5 or 7 sets.
